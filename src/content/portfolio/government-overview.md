**PH&C has been verified by CVE as a Service Disabled Veteran Owned Small Business (SDVOSB)**

PH&C works with Federal Transit Administration (FTA) as a consultant to Booze Allen & Hamilton to provide Project Management Oversite on FTA projects throughout the country. PH&C was charged with insuring government grant funds, such as American Recovery and Re-investment Act (ARRA) are being used for their grant purposes and in accordance with Federal Government regulations and standard industry practices. PH&C provides the person on the ground and the expertise on a variety of transit projects.
