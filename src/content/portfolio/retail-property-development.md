PH&C draws on vast experience from numerous projects with some of the nation’s and region’s most demanding and successful retailers, to include: [**AT&T**](https://www.att.com/), [**Burlington Coat Factory**](https://www.burlington.com/), [**Chic-Fil-A**](https://www.chick-fil-a.com/), [**CVS**](https://www.cvs.com/), [**Five Guys**](https://www.fiveguys.com/), [**Giant**](https://giantfoodstores.com/), **[Jersey Mike’s](https://www.jerseymikes.com/)**, [**Lidl US**](https://www.lidl.com/), [**Mattress Warehouse**](https://sleephappens.com/), [**Panda Express**](https://www.pandaexpress.com/), [**PNC Bank**](https://www.pnc.com/), [**Ross**](https://www.rossstores.com/), [**Safeway**](https://www.safeway.com/), **[ShopRite](https://shop.shoprite.com/store/d483123802)**, [**Staples**](https://www.staples.com/), [**Target**](https://www.target.com/), [**Verizon**](https://www.verizon.com/), [**Walgreens**](https://www.walgreens.com/), [**Wawa**](https://www.wawa.com/), [**Wegman’s**](https://www.wegmans.com/) and many more.

PH&C has also worked with a variety developers and REITs to reposition older shopping centers by overhauling the design, renovating façade and interior spaces, and re-tenanting their properties.
