Development of this former rock quarry and industrial parcel, along the busy commercial corridor of US Route 29 & the PA Turnpike in Malvern, is benefiting the community with retail food, convenience & fuel, health care, banking and a child care & educational institution. This valuable parcel of real estate is adjacent to a large newly built townhome and multi-family residential complex. Development required construction of sizable retaining walls, significant rock excavation, and sophisticated stormwater management features.   

**Key Features:**

*   Major rock removal (drilling and blasting) for site development.
*   New traffic signal, and road widening to the intersection of Morehall Road (Route 29) and the Pennsylvania Turnpike.
*   4,748 SF **[Wawa Food Market](https://www.wawa.com/)** with 12 fuel stations
*   10,000 SF Retail Building with **[Avola Kitchen + Bar](https://www.avolakitchenandbar.com/)** and available spaces
*   5,000 SF Retail Building with **[ATI Physical Therapy](https://locations.atipt.com/malvern-pa-n-morehall-rd)** and available spaces
*   2,778 SF **[TruMark Financial branch](https://www.trumarkonline.org/location/malvern/)**
*   13,200 SF **[Chesterbrook Academy](https://www.chesterbrookacademy.com/)** – See more details on our website **[HERE](https://phandc.net/chesterbrook-academy-private-preschool/)**
*   Individual NPDES Permit
*   PennDOT & Township access & signal improvements.
