Continuing our expansion into Residential and Multi-Family community projects, we assisted a team of investors develop this brand new 55+ age-qualified community located in the beautiful Saucon Valley of Pennsylvania.

Consisting of eighty classic cottages and a vibrant village center, The Cottages will be composed of simple sophistication for the discerning buyer which you can learn about by [CLICKING HERE.](https://oldsaucon.com/)

**Key Features:**

*   We procured and managed multiple contractors to handle sitework (on and off-site), land clearing, utility work, traffic signalization, landscape and hardscape features.
*   Developing this land included:
    *   Coordination with multiple governmental agencies and neighbors
    *   Installation, monitoring and maintenance of E&S controls
    *   Rock blasting
    *   425,000 CY of earth moving
    *   Value engineering, procurement and installation of:
        *   Traffic signal system at Rte 378 & Saucon Valley Road
        *   Stormwater irrigation system
        *   Landscape buffer installation
        *   Masonry entrance piers and signs
    *   Coordination of the dry utility work with UGI, PPL, Verizon, and Service Electric
